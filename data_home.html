My Data Journey

After a decade doing of qualitative research in Japanese and English, I began to expand my skillsets into quantitative research, particularly into network analysis, demographic analysis, and consumer trends. During my 2018-19 postdoc at Harvard, I learned some of the basics of text mining in R and using Palladio to map out business and diplomatic networks.

I enhanced this skillset by taking the Google Data Analytics Course, where I learned how to turn raw, messy data into actionable insights. During the course and since, I used Tableau to create compelling data visualizations, SQL queries to clean data and identify key data trends.

Most recently, I have been focusing on data packages in Python and learning how to interface with APIs like the Spotify API to discover geographic expressions of consumer preferences.
